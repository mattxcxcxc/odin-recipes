Lasgna Recipe

Description

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets. The same-named Italian dish is made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices . A great delicacy and very popular for dinner time eatings.

Ingredients

  • Itaian Sausage
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
